Our story

We opened to keep one good habit.

Cadence started in 2019 with a six-group lever machine, a borrowed grinder, and a simple idea: the same careful cup, at the same time, every day.

We roast a few miles away in small batches and bring it to the bar within a week of the roast date. Our baristas weigh every shot and taste the batch brew each hour, because consistency isn't boring — it's the whole point. A regular order should taste like it did yesterday.
